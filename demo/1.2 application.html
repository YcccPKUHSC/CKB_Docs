Apply for project

CKB Official10/17/25About 6 minguide

After successful account registration, you can log in to the CKB Cloud Platform using email verification. Click the Applications tab at the top of the page to enter the project application interface.

Participants filter (optional)

Tips

To help you streamline your dataset and reduce storage costs, the platform offers an optional Object Filtering feature. You can set filtering criteria for specific fields to create a smaller, more targeted participant subset that meets your research needs.

  1. Click the Add filter button on the right side of the page, and the system will pop up the filter condition configuration dialog.

  2. This dialog is divided into three functional areas:

    • Left: Displays a four-level classification of all CKB data fields in a tree structure.

    • Middle: When you select a specific field on the left, this area will show detailed information about the field, including its section, field description, and a chart displaying the distribution of its values.

    • Right: Configure specific filter conditions for the field in this area.

  3. After configuring the filter conditions for the field, click the Add button at the bottom left of the dialog to add this condition to the filter condition pool.

Add variable

Tips

To ensure data security and help minimize the costs associated with data requests and storage, please only request the specific variables necessary for your actual research needs.

  1. Click the Add Variables button on the right side of the page, and the system will pop up the variable selection dialog.

  2. This dialog is divided into three functional areas:

    • Left: Displays a four-level classification of all CKB data fields in a tree structure, allowing you to check the desired fields (select all or individual ones).

    • Middle: When you select a specific field on the left, this area will show detailed information about the field, including its section, field description, and a chart displaying the distribution of its values.

    • Right: Selected fields can be added collectively to the variable pool in this area.

  3. After checking the specific fields, click the Confirm button at the bottom left of the dialog to add all selected fields to the variable pool.

Review the intended data request list

Tips

Please carefully review the final data list generated by the system before submitting your project application. Once submitted, the data scope will be difficult to modify. If you identify any omissions or unnecessary selections, be sure to adjust your filter conditions and variable choices before submission to avoid impacting the subsequent project review process and your research progress.

The intended data request list consists of four parts:

  • Serial Number: The data list number.

  • Filter Conditions: Displays selected fields and conditions as independent modules, supporting individual deletion.

  • Filter Results: Shows the number of research subjects meeting the criteria.

  • Operations: Provides options to "Add Filter Conditions" and "Add Variables," and supports deletion of this data list.

Project application process

After confirming the data list is correct, you can click the Project application button in the upper right corner of the page to enter the project application process. The progress bar at the top of the page displays the workflow steps, including: 1 Data filters - 2 Project detail - 3 Project member - 4 Preview - 5 Submitted.

Select data scope

Under the 1 Data filters process:

  1. Select the data types based on the actual selected data, including imaging, whole genome sequencing, and phenotype categories.

  2. Choose the data lists according to project requirements. Multiple selections are allowed.

Click the Next button at the bottom of the page to proceed to the next step.

Fill in project details

Under the 2 Project detail process, please complete the following application information based on your project research plan:

  1. Project Title: Provide a title that accurately summarizes the research content.

  2. Project Summary: Outline the background of the project, core research questions, specific research objectives, and its potential impact on public health.

  3. Scientific Rationale: Explain the scientific basis of the project, such as relevant background in the field or any preliminary pilot studies.

  4. Research Methods: Briefly describe the experimental design, analysis plan, and the specific use of CKB data in the project.

  5. Expected Project Outputs: Clearly specify the following two points:

    • Whether this study will generate new data or results of value to other researchers.

    • The anticipated publications from this study (you may indicate the intended journals for submission).

  6. Keywords: Enter individual keywords in the input box and press Enter to confirm. Repeat this process to add multiple keywords.

  7. Ethical Review: Indicate whether this project requires ethical review based on the regulations of your country/region.

  8. Project Funding: List all funding sources and the names of the funding institutions.

  9. Project Timeline: Provide an overview of the expected schedule and milestones for the project.

  10. Supplementary Materials (Optional): Upload PDF files (such as documents, charts, etc.) to support your project application.

Click the  Next button at the bottom of the page to proceed to the next step.

Add project members

Under the 3 Project members process:

Please enter the institutional email addresses associated with the project members' CKB cloud platform accounts. The system will automatically search and match users based on their email addresses, adding them to the project member list. Repeat this process to add multiple project members.

Click the Next button at the bottom of the page to proceed to the next step.

Preview the application sheet

Under the 4 Preview process:

The system has automatically generated the project application form based on the information you provided. This is the final step. Please carefully review the accuracy of all information and ensure that the intended data request list fully aligns with your research needs.

If you need to modify the application, you can click the Previous button at the bottom of the page multiple times to return to previous steps and make adjustments.

Once everything is confirmed to be correct, click the Submit button at the bottom of the page to formally submit your project application.

Pending review

Tips

To ensure data security and the uniqueness of all research projects, the CKB team will review your project application. If any adjustments are needed, we will provide detailed optimization suggestions to help you refine your proposal and resubmit the application.

After submitting your project application, the CKB team will review your project and provide feedback on the review result within 14 business days.

You can find your submitted project and its review status under the My Projects tab.

Review Status:

  • Pending Review: Please wait patiently for the review.
  • Approved: Your project application has passed the review. The next steps include uploading the ethical approval document, signing the data agreement, and completing the payment.
  • Rejected: Your project application requires optimization. Please check the optimization suggestions, revise the application content, and resubmit your application.

Submission and Review of Materials

Tips

To ensure data security and research compliance, you are required to submit the Ethical Approval Document (issued by your institution) and the Signed Data Use Agreement.

After submission, the CKB team will review your documents. If any supplements or modifications are needed, we will provide clear guidance to assist you in completing the material preparation successfully.

After your project application is approved, you must also submit the following two required documents:

  • 📑 Ethical Review Approval Document (issued by your institution)

  • 📑 Signed Data Use Agreement

After submission, the CKB team will review your documents and provide feedback on the review result within 7 business days.

You can find your submitted project and check the document review status under the My Projects tab.

Document Review Status:

  • Pending Review: Please wait patiently for the review.
  • Approved: Your project documents have passed the review. The next step is to complete the payment.
  • Rejected: Your submitted documents require modifications. Please check the revision suggestions and re-upload the corresponding materials.

Fee Payment

Tips

To ensure you continue to receive stable, high-quality CKB data services, corresponding fees will be charged based on your data usage scope. These fees will be entirely used to support the operation, maintenance, and updates of the CKB system and the database itself. As CKB is a non-profit project, your support is crucial for the sustainability of this valuable scientific resource.

After both the project and supporting documents are approved, you will need to pay the data usage fee. The specific process is as follows:

  1. Receive the Invoice: The system will generate an invoice based on the approved scope of data usage and send it to your account. You can find the corresponding project and view the invoice details on the My Projects page.

  2. Complete the Payment: After verifying the invoice details are correct, please click the Pay button next to the invoice and follow the instructions to complete the payment.

  3. Obtain Proof of Payment: Upon successful payment, the system will automatically generate and provide a PDF format payment receipt and official invoice for your records and reimbursement purposes.

Data Release

Tips

Congratulations! You have now completed all the steps of the project application process and can begin your data analysis.

Please note that CKB data will be released directly to the DCS Cloud Platform and is no longer available for local download. Please log in to the platform to perform data analysis and export your results in the cloud.

After completing all steps of the project application process, the CKB data you applied for will be released directly to the DCS Cloud Platform.

You can find your project under the My Projects tab. If the DCS Cloud column shows Successful, it indicates the data has been successfully released and you may begin your analysis.
